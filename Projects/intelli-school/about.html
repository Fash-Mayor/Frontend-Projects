Our Story

Founded in 1999, Academy has been a beacon of educational excellence for over two decades. What started as a small institution with a vision to transform education has grown into one of the most respected schools in the region. Our journey has been marked by a commitment to innovation, inclusivity, and excellence in every aspect of education.

Today, we serve over 1,500 students from diverse backgrounds, guided by a faculty of over 80 dedicated educators. We continue to build on our legacy while embracing modern pedagogies and technologies to prepare our students for the challenges of tomorrow.

Our Mission

To provide a comprehensive, innovative, and inclusive education that develops critical thinking, creativity, and compassion in our students. We strive to create an environment where every individual can discover their potential and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence that nurtures globally-minded, socially-conscious individuals who are equipped with the knowledge, skills, and values to excel in a rapidly changing world and make a positive impact on their communities.
