Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements?

Students should have completed their previous level of education. We review academic transcripts, entrance exams, and conduct interviews as part of the admission process.

When are the enrollment periods?

Primary enrollment occurs in March through May for the following academic year. Late admissions are considered on a rolling basis based on seat availability.

What scholarships are available?

We offer merit-based and need-based scholarships. Students can apply for scholarships during the admission process. Financial aid advisors can discuss various options.

Do you provide transportation?

Yes, we have a comprehensive transportation system. Multiple pickup and drop-off points are available throughout the city at an additional fee.

What extracurricular activities are offered?

We offer over 30 clubs and activities including sports, arts, science clubs, and community service programs. Students can participate in one or more activities.

How can I schedule a campus tour?

You can schedule a tour by contacting our admissions office directly or filling out the form on this page. Tours are available Monday through Friday between 10 AM and 3 PM.
